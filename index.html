about me

What's up! I'm Tarik, an aspiring software developer based out of Miami, interested in learning the ins, outs, and limits of modern web/software development. I'm a recent graduate of Hamilton College where I majored in Africana Studies and minored in Japanese language. As indicative of my studies, I avidly consume Black urban culture (yes! Hip-hop!), which at various points intersects with my more modest interests in Japanese culture. These range from Japanese manga (the likes of Takehiko Inoue's Vagabond) to modern Japanese minimalism and urban youth culture. Being a 90s baby of the internet generation, all of my personal interests are intimately influenced, and mediated by the web. I work to hone my skills as a developer to not only bring myself among those who architect this powerfully mediating force, but to befriend and work alongside individuals who share a passion for creating and authoring content and software that brings people across the world together in the best ways possible.
