about me

I'm a software developer based out of Miami, passionate about learning the ins, outs, and limits of modern web/software development. I avidly consume Black urban culture (yes! Hip-hop!), which at various points intersects with my more modest interests in Japanese culture (as indicative of the video above).

Being a 90s baby of the internet generation, all of my personal interests are intimately influenced, and mediated by the web. I work to hone my skills as a developer to not only establish myself as an architect of this powerfully mediating force, but to befriend and work alongside individuals who share a passion for creating and authoring content and software that brings people across the world together in the best ways possible.

genius-bot

Genius Bot is a responsive discord bot that uses the LyricsGenius framework to scrape song data and other artist metadata from the Genius website via the Genius web API. At it's most basic level, the Bot can recite the lyrics for your favorite songs. With more nuanced queries, the bot can provide various info about an artist's catalog of music.
